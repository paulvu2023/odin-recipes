Chao Mein

Description

Embark on a culinary journey to the heart of China with Chow Mein, where the captivating fusion of egg noodles and vibrant stir-fried vegetables awaits. Savor the succulence of our favorite addition, tender chicken, or explore a world of possibilities with alternative meats or the velvety embrace of tofu. Watch in awe as the noodles sizzle and crisp, creating a tantalizing texture, before being drenched in an irresistible sauce. With minimal cleanup and a single pan, this dish brings simplicity to your kitchen while delivering a symphony of flavors that will transport your taste buds to new heights. Let Chow Mein be your passport to a delightful evening, where culinary mastery meets convenience and indulgence reigns supreme.

Ingredients

  • Chao Mein Noodles
  • Vegetables: Carrots, cabbage, green onions, and bean sprouts
  • Meats: chicken, beef, shrimp, pork
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chicken Broth
  • Corn Starch
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. In a small mixing bowl, combine ingredients for chow mein sauce: oyster sauce, sesame oil, soy sauce, chicken broth, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions, set aside.
  3. Heat a large wok or pan. Add a bit of oil to the pan and cook chicken until itâ€™s golden brown. Remove chicken and set aside.
  4. Add carrots, cabbage and pressed garlic and saute for a few minutes until veggies are slightly softened.
  5. Add chicken back to the pan, followed by cooked noodles and pour the sauce right on top. Cook for about 2 minutes, distributing all that saucy goodness around evenly.
  6. Add chopped green onions and remove from the heat. Serve hot!
